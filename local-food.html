~*~ Surabaya's Culinary Delights! ~*~

Makanan Surabaya!

Surabaya has loads of must try foods, because of its unique location, the food scene is Surabaya is incredibly diverse. That said, Surabaya also has a bunch of local foods you have to try.

Semanggi Suroboyo

Semanggi Suroboyo is a very unique and traditional salad. The main ingredient is boiled semanggi (clover) leaves, which are mixed with bean sprouts. The real star is the sauce! It's a sweet and savory dressing made from sweet potato and peanuts. It's usually served on a banana leaf and eaten with krupuk puli (rice crackers). Semanggi Suroboyo holds a special place in Surabaya, so much so there's even a bus line named "Semanggi"

Rawon Setan

Now for something legendary... Rawon Setan! Rawon is a rich, black beef soup. The deep black color comes from the keluak nut, which gives it a uniquely nutty and savory flavor. The name "Setan" (which means devil) was given to this specific stall because it originally opened late at night and was famous for its spicy kick! It's the perfect meal for a late-night food adventure.
