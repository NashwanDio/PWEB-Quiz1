~*~ Come Visit Surabaya's Best Spots! ~*~

Don't forget your camera!

Ready to explore? Surabaya has so many cool places to see, from modern streets to historical corners. Here are two must-visit destinations that show the different sides of the city.

Jalan Tunjungan (Tunjungan Street)

This is the historic heart of modern Surabaya! Jalan Tunjungan is one of the most iconic streets in the city, famous for its beautiful blend of new shops and old Dutch colonial architecture. It's the perfect place for an evening stroll. The street is lined with cafes, boutiques, and historic hotels. It's so famous, there's even a classic song about it, "Rek Ayo Rek Mlaku-Mlaku nang Tunjungan" (Hey friends, let's go for a walk on Tunjungan).

Kota Tua (Old Town)

If you want to step back in time, you have to visit Kota Tua, or the Old Town area. This part of the city, centered around the famous Jembatan Merah (Red Bridge), was the original trading hub. You'll find old warehouses, offices, and buildings with stunning Dutch, Chinese, and Arabian architectural influences. It's a fantastic area for photography and learning about the city's rich history. Don't miss the House of Sampoerna, a fascinating museum in the area!
